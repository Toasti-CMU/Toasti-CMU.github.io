TOASTI stands for Tongue Operated ASsisTive Instrument.

Mission Statement

We are a project group committed to the development of TOASTI, an assistive technology meant to help patients with paralysis in communicating with others.

Our Device

Our device transmits a signal from an EMG sensor placed on your skin (A) to your computer (B), which can then facilitate conversations (C) or send emergency help (D).
